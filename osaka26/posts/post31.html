MY EQUESTRIAN LIFE

I watched more videos of horses and then seriously thought ‘I’m gonna get a horse when I get back to the Midwest.’ Upon further thought, I realized that I’m actually not that dedicated to care for a horse for 30 years or however long a horse lives to be. I also don’t have any money. I also don’t know anything about horses. So, I utilized the second best option to conjure this dream: my imagination. I thought of a life where I’m a Chicago barista in the morning, then an equestrian at night.

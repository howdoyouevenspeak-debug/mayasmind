BLACKHEAD PROGRESSION
I’m sure you all were waiting for this moment, so as promised, here is the progression of my blackhead:

  • (warning: GROSS!)
  • 1/8

    This blackhead has been on my face since February of this year. I thought it was gonna eventually go away on it’s own, but it proved to be staying for the long run until about three days ago when it began hurting.
  • 2/8

    Took a tweezer to it but only got the top.
  • 3/8

    That night, it began turning red and hurting again.
  • 4/8

    Morning after putting the tweezer to it, it looked like a crater. I felt so embarrassed to go out, so I tried finding a way to rid of it. Maybe I needed a cream, an acid or something. Suddenly, I felt a primitive urge to just squeeze out whatever was in there. I didn’t know if I would find anything, but luckily a salmon roe-sized plug came out covered in white pus. It was the most satisfying experience of my life. I should have taken a picture, but I played around with it until I squished it with my fingernail.
  • 5/8

    Put a bandaid on the wound to prevent oils from getting on it. Looked like an anime wannabe walking around.
  • 6/8

    When I asked google if the blackhead is gonna come back, it said yeah. I assumed this was the formation of the next blackhead, but it was just the scab.
  • 7/8

    I removed the scab that was forming.
  • FINALE!


    It’s healing over now. it’s strange not having that blackhead on my face anymore. I thought it was eventually gonna grow into a huge mole that I’d have to remove when I’m 50. It feels so smooth now. Nice.

    I wonder why that blackhead formed on my face in the first place. Was my face just that dirty? Why was it on my face for months as opposed to weeks? Why did it randomly begin hurting? So many questions.

    It might be my skincare routine. I think I have nice skin overall, but because I use a bunch of lotion-type things, it’s more difficult for my pore to flush properly. Hence, I got an oil based cleanser to clear the oil from my face. I hope that keeps the blackheads away. It also might have been the placement. Maybe because it was an inconvenient place for a pimple to form, the cartilage of my nose. Thank goodness I’m in Japan where there are reliable skincare products galore. Seriously, my skin has never looked better:

    Incredible.

    I’m not gonna put any more thought into it. It was genuinely the most minuscule issue. My main lesson learned is to not listen to Google’s AI overview because they’re probably being paid by doctors. They told me not to remove it, yet I did, and now I’m the happiest I’ve ever been. Removing it would have probably been $100 at a dermatologist. ANYWAY, that was the progression of my blackhead. I thought it was interesting.

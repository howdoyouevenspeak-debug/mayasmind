SKINS YAP
Actually, I WILL talk about skins because this is MAYA’SMIND™ and MAYA’SMIND™ is a place of FREEDOM!

Skins is a good show if you haven’t watched it. It’s about a bunch of British teenagers in the same social circle, and every episode centers around one person of the group. I just finished the episode about Naomi who turned out to be shamefully gay, which was a fun twist because the girl she’s in love with is openly gay.

The thing about skins is that everyone is always hooking up with one another. Like, I don’t think there’s been a single episode without an intimate scene, even with the people you’d never expect together like the openly gay girl + the autistic guy. I guess 2008 British teenagers were crazy like that.
